Welcome to the 2024 Anderson Family Reunion

Event Details

Reunion Costs: $30 for an Individual, $50 for a Family

Date: October 19th, 2024

Start Time: 11:00 am CST

Food Serving: 1:00-3:00pm CST

Location: Smithville VFW

557 N W Loop 230, Smithville, TX 78957

This year's reunion continues the tradition of connection, food and fun for the WHOLE family. Including competitive games like dominoes, spades and more.

Delicious Southern Food

This year's menu will feature melt in your mouth brisket, delicious sausage, and just right chicken with delicious greens, chili beans, salads, and all types of handmade desserts from family members.

Showcase your skills and bring a side dish (if you can't cook, grab one of your favorite sides from the store and share it with us).

Entertainment

DJ Big Baby will be on the turntables once again, keeping the vibe right for dancing and overall musical enjoyment.

Raffle Prizes

This year we are raffling off a new 50 inch Hisense Smart TV and a 12x12 metal Pavilion.

You can pre-purchase as many raffles tickets as you want through Tilda or buy them in-person at the reunion.

See y'all there!
